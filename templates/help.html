Travel class (social class / ticket type):

1 = First class (luxury)

2 = Second class

3 = Third class (cheap ticket)

Indicates social status – the wealthiest often survived longer

sex: Passenger gender. One of the strongest survival factors – women were prioritized during evacuation.

Age: Age in years

Number of siblings or spouse on board and Number of parents/children on board:

On the Titanic, women and children were taken on board first, often with their families in lifeboats. If you had a family on board, you were more likely to be “in a group” that boarded a lifeboat. The passengers who were completely alone were more likely to be men in third class—a group with a very low chance of survival.

Ticket price:

Ticket price in British pounds. A higher value usually means first class.

Who:

Summarizes age and gender:

man = male over 18

woman = female over 18

child = child under 18

adult_male:

True = adult male

False = woman or child
